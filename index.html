Welcome to 2048

In the 18th century, a mathematician named Professor Fibonacci discovered the Numerical Convergence Matrix, a mystical grid capable of unifying numbers into a perfect entity: the Golden Tile (2048).

Despite his brilliance, Fibonacci was unable to solve the puzzle, leaving it as a challenge for future generations. Now, as a modern mathematician, you must slide and merge tiles to unlock the secrets of the Golden Tile.

Prove yourself as one of the greatest minds in history and solve the puzzle that has eluded mathematicians for centuries.

How to Play

Objective: Slide numbered tiles on the grid using your arrow keys. When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into a single tile with a value equal to their sum. Your goal is to create a tile with the value 2048 to win the game!

Power-Ups

  • Undo: Revert your last move.
  • Shuffle: Randomize the positions of all tiles on the grid.
  • Teleport: Move a specific tile to an empty cell of your choice.
  • Bomb: Remove a random tile from the grid.
  • Delete Tile: Select and delete a specific tile of your choice.
