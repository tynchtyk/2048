How to Play

Objective: Slide numbered tiles on the grid using your arrow keys. When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into a single tile with a value equal to their sum. Your goal is to create a tile with the value 2048 to win the game!

Power-Ups

  • Undo: Revert your last move.
  • Shuffle: Randomize the positions of all tiles on the grid.
  • Teleport: Move a specific tile to an empty cell of your choice.
  • Bomb: Remove a random tile from the grid.
  • Delete Tile: Select and delete a specific tile of your choice.
